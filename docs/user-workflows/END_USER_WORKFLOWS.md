# InTime End User Workflows

Plain English requirements for an AI-native staffing platform.

---

## Who Uses This System?

### 1. Recruiter
The person who finds candidates and matches them to jobs. Their success is measured by placements.

### 2. Account Manager
The person who manages client relationships. Their success is measured by client retention and growth.

### 3. Sales Rep
The person who brings in new clients. Their success is measured by new business revenue.

### 4. Operations / Coordinator
The person who handles logistics - timesheets, compliance, onboarding. Their success is measured by accuracy and timeliness.

### 5. Manager
The person who oversees a team. Their success is measured by team performance.

### 6. Executive
The person who runs the business. Their success is measured by revenue, margin, and growth.

### 7. System Admin
The person who keeps the system running, secure, and configured correctly. Their success is measured by uptime, security, and user satisfaction.

## RECRUITER WORKFLOWS

### R1: Starting My Day

**What happens today:**
- Open email, check for client responses
- Open ATS, check submission statuses
- Open calendar, see today's interviews
- Open spreadsheet, check pipeline
- Open Slack, see if anyone needs anything
- Spend 30 minutes figuring out what to do first

**What should happen:**
- Open InTime
- See exactly what needs attention right now
- See what's overdue and why
- See what opportunities exist today
- Know the single most important thing to do first

**Success criteria:**
- Within 30 seconds of opening the app, I know my priorities
- Nothing falls through the cracks
- I don't have to remember what to do - the system tells me

---

### R2: A Client Gives Me a New Job to Fill

**What happens today:**
- Get email or call from client with job details
- Manually type job into ATS
- Search database for matching candidates
- Review dozens of profiles
- Make a list of who might fit
- Start reaching out one by one

**What should happen:**
- Enter basic job info (title, key requirements)
- System suggests complete job description based on similar roles
- System instantly shows candidates who match
- System ranks them by fit and explains why
- I review the top matches and decide who to pursue

**Success criteria:**
- Job is created in under 5 minutes
- I see qualified candidates immediately
- I understand why each candidate matches
- I don't miss good candidates in my own database

### R3: Submitting a Candidate to a Client

**What happens today:**
- Find candidate profile
- Download/update resume
- Write submission email

**Success criteria:**
- Submission takes under 2 minutes
