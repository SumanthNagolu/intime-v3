# New Hire Guide

Welcome to InTime! This guide will help you get started.

---

## Before Day 1

Your manager will send you:
- [ ] Offer letter (sign and return)
- [ ] Background check authorization
- [ ] Equipment preferences (if applicable)
- [ ] Start date and time confirmation

---

## Day 1 Checklist

### Morning (First 2 hours)

**HR & Admin**
- [ ] Complete employment paperwork
- [ ] Submit ID and tax documents
- [ ] Set up direct deposit
- [ ] Review benefits enrollment (if applicable)

**System Access**
- [ ] Receive company email
- [ ] Join Slack workspace
- [ ] Access InTime platform
- [ ] Set up 2FA on all accounts

**Orientation**
- [ ] Meet your team lead
- [ ] Tour the workspace (virtual or physical)
- [ ] Review team schedule and meeting times
- [ ] Get added to calendar invites

### Afternoon (Next 2 hours)

**Required Reading**
- [ ] [Company Bible](../01-foundation/COMPANY_BIBLE.md) - Our values and culture
- [ ] [Business Model](../02-business-model/BUSINESS_MODEL.md) - How we make money
- [ ] Your [Role Handbook](../05-handbooks/) - Your specific guide

**Meet Your Team**
- [ ] 1:1 with team lead (30 min)
- [ ] Introduction to team members
- [ ] Understand team structure and responsibilities

---

## Week 1 Schedule

### Day 1: Orientation
- HR paperwork and setup
- Required reading
- Meet your team

### Day 2-3: Learning
- Complete [US IT Staffing 101](../06-training/guides/US_IT_STAFFING_101.md)
- Shadow team members
- Attend team meetings

### Day 4-5: Practice
- Start with supervised tasks
- Ask questions (no question is dumb)
- Review processes with buddy
