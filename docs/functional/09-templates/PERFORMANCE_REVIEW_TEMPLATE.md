## Appendix: Rating Scale Guide

| Rating | Label | Definition |
|--------|-------|------------|
| 5 | Exceptional | Far exceeds expectations consistently. Performance is outstanding and exemplary. Serves as a role model. |
| 4 | Exceeds | Regularly exceeds expectations. Performance is above what is required. Shows initiative and impact beyond the role. |
| 3 | Meets | Fully meets expectations. Performance is solid and reliable. Delivers what is expected consistently. |
| 2 | Developing | Partially meets expectations. Some areas need improvement. May be new to role or facing challenges. |
| 1 | Below | Does not meet expectations. Significant improvement needed. May require performance improvement plan. |
