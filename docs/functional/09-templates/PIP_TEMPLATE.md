## Section 7: Consequences

### If Goals Are Met

- PIP will be closed successfully
- Employee returns to regular performance management
- Improvement will be noted in performance record
- Continued performance at this level expected

### If Goals Are Partially Met

- PIP may be extended (up to 30 additional days)
- Specific remaining issues will be identified
- New milestones will be set
- Continued support provided

### If Goals Are Not Met

- Employment may be terminated
- Final decision will be made by [Manager + HR/Leadership]
- Applicable notice period will apply
- Final paycheck and benefits per policy

## Appendix: Tips for Success

### For Employees

1. Take the PIP seriously
2. Ask clarifying questions
3. Document your progress
4. Communicate proactively
5. Ask for help when needed
6. Meet all check-in appointments
7. Be honest about challenges

### For Managers

1. Be clear and specific
2. Provide genuine support
3. Document everything
4. Hold regular check-ins
5. Give real-time feedback
6. Be fair and consistent
7. Make tough decisions when needed

---

*A PIP is an opportunity for improvement, not just documentation for termination. Invest in success.*
