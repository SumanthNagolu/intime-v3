## Quick Reference

### Good 1:1 Practices

**For Managers:**
- Be present and focused
- Listen more than talk
- Ask open-ended questions
- Give specific feedback
- Follow up on action items

**For Team Members:**
- Come prepared
- Be honest about challenges
- Ask for what you need
- Take notes
- Own your development

### Questions to Ask

**About Work:**
- What's going well?
- What's frustrating you?
- What do you need to be more effective?

**About Growth:**
- What do you want to learn?
- Where do you want to be in 1-2 years?
- How can I help you grow?

**About Team:**
- How's collaboration with the team?
- Anyone you'd like to work more with?
- Any team dynamics to address?

**About Me (Manager):**
- What can I do better?
- Am I giving you enough context?
- How can I support you more?
