# Client Materials Library

**InTime Sales & Marketing Documentation**

## Overview

This directory contains outbound marketing and sales materials designed for prospective clients, sales meetings, and ongoing account management. These materials communicate InTime's value proposition, service offerings, and differentiation.

## Contents

### CAPABILITY_OVERVIEW.md

**Primary purpose:** Company overview, capabilities, and pitch document for prospective clients.

**Use cases:**
- Initial discovery meeting: Share overview of InTime before detailed conversations
- Proposal documents: Include or reference in client proposals
- Website/collateral: Adapt for website, marketing materials, and email campaigns
- Sales deck support: Provide narrative and talking points for sales presentations
- Ongoing communication: Reference for quarterly business reviews and account planning

**Key sections:**
- Company overview and mission
- Six key differentiators
- Complete service lines (contract, contract-to-hire, direct hire, managed services, training)
- Deep technology specializations
- 6-step sales and delivery process
- Client success metrics and targets
- Compliance and quality standards
- Call-to-action and contact information

**Customization notes:**
- Replace [PLACEHOLDER] fields with actual contact information, URLs, and team names

## Using These Materials

### For Sales Team

1. **Discovery Meetings:** Share Capability Overview to establish credibility and service breadth
2. **Proposal Preparation:** Reference service descriptions and metrics when writing proposals
3. **Competitive Positioning:** Use differentiators section to distinguish InTime from competitors
4. **Client Education:** Use detailed technology sections to explain capabilities
5. **Follow-Up:** Send Capability Overview via email after first call to keep InTime top-of-mind

### For Account Management

1. **Quarterly Reviews:** Reference client success metrics to demonstrate partnership value
2. **Upsell Conversations:** Use service line descriptions to identify expansion opportunities
3. **Relationship Building:** Share relevant case studies or industry insights from materials
4. **Retention:** Use mission statement and values to remind clients of partnership principles

### For Marketing

1. **Website Content:** Adapt for "Services," "Why Choose Us," and "How We Work" pages
2. **Email Campaigns:** Use value propositions and differentiators in outreach sequences
3. **Collateral Creation:** Build one-pagers, brochures, and case studies from this framework
4. **LinkedIn/Social:** Extract quotes and key stats for social media content
5. **Sales Enablement:** Create battlecards comparing InTime to specific competitors

### For Operations

1. **Client Onboarding:** Share Capability Overview during MSA negotiation
2. **Expectation Setting:** Reference service level targets and compliance standards
3. **Documentation:** Use as reference for what services are promised to clients
4. **Training:** Reference for new employee training on InTime's offerings
