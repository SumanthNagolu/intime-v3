# Intern 12-Week Training Program

Structured curriculum for transforming interns into productive team members.

---

## Program Overview

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Duration | 12 weeks |
| Format | On-the-job + structured learning |
| Goal | 100% productivity by Week 12 |
| Audience | New interns (recruiters, coordinators) |

---

## Learning Path

```
Week 1-2: Foundation
Week 3-4: Core Skills
Week 5-6: Practice (Supervised)
Week 7-8: Practice (Semi-independent)
Week 9-10: Advanced Skills
Week 11-12: Full Productivity
```

---

## Week 1-2: Foundation

### Goal
Understand the industry, company, and role basics.

### Topics

| Day | Topic | Activities |
|-----|-------|------------|
| 1 | Orientation | HR, systems, introductions |
| 2 | Company overview | Company Bible, values, culture |
| 3 | Industry intro | US IT Staffing 101 (Ch 1-2) |
| 4 | Industry cont. | US IT Staffing 101 (Ch 3-4) |
| 5 | Role overview | Read role handbook |
| 6 | Tools training | System access, basic navigation |
| 7 | Tools practice | Hands-on with guidance |
| 8 | Immigration basics | Visa types, work authorization |
| 9 | Recap and Q&A | Review, clarify doubts |
| 10 | Shadow Day 1 | Observe senior team member |

### Deliverables

- [ ] Complete US IT Staffing 101
- [ ] Pass basics quiz (80%+)
- [ ] System access working
- [ ] Shadow notes from observation

### Success Criteria

- Can explain what InTime does
- Understands basic visa types
- Navigates systems without help
- Asks good questions

---

## Week 3-4: Core Skills

### Goal
Learn the fundamental skills of the role.

### Topics (Recruiters)

| Day | Topic | Activities |
|-----|-------|------------|
| 1-2 | Boolean search | Learn, practice |
| 3-4 | Sourcing | Job boards, LinkedIn |
| 5-6 | Screening calls | Observe, then practice |
| 7-8 | Qualifying candidates | BANT, skills assessment |
| 9-10 | Database management | Entering, updating records |

### Topics (Coordinators)

| Day | Topic | Activities |
|-----|-------|------------|
| 1-2 | Scheduling | Calendar, coordination |
| 3-4 | Documentation | Templates, processes |
| 5-6 | Communication | Email, phone etiquette |
| 7-8 | Tracking | Dashboards, reporting |
| 9-10 | Multi-tasking | Handling priorities |

### Deliverables

- [ ] Complete 5 practice searches (recruiters)
- [ ] Complete 3 mock screens (recruiters)
- [ ] Schedule 3 mock calls (coordinators)
- [ ] Process 5 sample requests (coordinators)

### Success Criteria

- Performs core tasks with supervision
- Understands quality standards
- Follows processes correctly
- Quality is acceptable (may be slow)

---

## Week 5-6: Practice (Supervised)

### Goal
Handle real work with close supervision.

### Activities

| Week | Focus | Supervision |
|------|-------|-------------|
| 5 | Real tasks, full review | Manager reviews 100% |
| 6 | Real tasks, spot check | Manager reviews 50% |

### For Recruiters

- Source 20+ candidates (real)
- Conduct 5+ screening calls (observed)
- Submit 3+ candidates to database
- Get feedback on each task

### For Coordinators

- Schedule 10+ real meetings/calls
- Process 10+ requests
- Create 5+ documents
- Get feedback on each task

### Deliverables

- [ ] Complete assigned tasks
- [ ] Achieve 70%+ quality score
- [ ] Document learnings
- [ ] Identify improvement areas

### Success Criteria

- Completes tasks independently (with review)
- Quality improving week over week
- Takes feedback constructively
- Proactively asks questions

---

## Week 7-8: Practice (Semi-independent)

### Goal
Work more independently with spot checks.

### Activities

| Week | Focus | Supervision |
|------|-------|-------------|
| 7 | Increased volume | Manager reviews 25% |
| 8 | Near-full workload | Manager reviews 10% |

### For Recruiters

- Source 40+ candidates
- Conduct 10+ screens
- Submit 5+ qualified candidates
- Handle 1-2 hot requirements

### For Coordinators

- Full task ownership
- Handle routine requests solo
- Flag issues appropriately
- Support team proactively

### Deliverables

- [ ] Meet 70% of full targets
- [ ] Achieve 80%+ quality score
- [ ] Resolve 3+ issues independently
- [ ] 8-week review with manager

### Success Criteria

- Handles routine work independently
- Knows when to escalate
- Quality meets standards
- Volume approaching targets

---

## Week 9-10: Advanced Skills

### Goal
Learn advanced skills and handle complex scenarios.

### Topics (Recruiters)

| Topic | Focus |
|-------|-------|
| Complex searches | Hard-to-find skills |
| Difficult screens | Handling objections |
| Passive candidates | Engagement strategies |
| Multi-req management | Priority juggling |
| Quality improvement | Reducing errors |

### Topics (Coordinators)

| Topic | Focus |
|-------|-------|
| Complex scheduling | Multi-party, urgent |
| Process improvement | Efficiency tips |
| Reporting | Creating reports |
| Client communication | Professional handling |
| Crisis management | Handling problems |

### Deliverables

- [ ] Handle 2+ complex scenarios
- [ ] Propose 1 process improvement
- [ ] Achieve 85%+ quality score
- [ ] Meet 85% of targets

### Success Criteria

- Handles complex work
- Minimal supervision needed
- Proactive problem-solving
- Helps newer interns

---

## Week 11-12: Full Productivity

### Goal
Achieve full productivity as a team member.

### Expectations

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Workload | 100% of role expectations |
| Quality | 90%+ |
| Supervision | <5% review needed |
| Independence | Full |

### Activities

- Full workload ownership
- Mentor observation of newer interns
- 12-week final review
- Graduation celebration

### Deliverables

- [ ] Meet 100% of role targets
- [ ] Achieve 90%+ quality score
- [ ] Complete final assessment
- [ ] Receive graduation certification

### Success Criteria

- Fully productive team member
- Quality work independently
- Reliable and consistent
- Ready for next growth step

|------|------------|

## Evaluation Criteria

### Technical Skills (40%)

- Task completion
- Quality of work
- Speed and efficiency
- Tool proficiency

### Professional Skills (30%)

- Communication
- Time management
- Problem-solving
- Attention to detail

### Cultural Fit (30%)

- Teamwork
- Initiative
- Attitude
- Values alignment

---

## Post-Program

### Successful Completion

- Assigned to team permanently
- Full role responsibilities
- Regular 1:1s continue
- Career development planning

### Extended Training

If not ready at Week 12:
- Identify specific gaps
- Create improvement plan
- Extend training 2-4 weeks
- Re-assess at end

### Performance Concerns

If significant issues:
- Document concerns
- Discuss with HR
- Consider role fit
- Make appropriate decision
