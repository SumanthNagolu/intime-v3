# Compensation Policy

InTime's approach to salaries, incentives, and total rewards.

---

## Philosophy

Our compensation philosophy is built on these principles:

1. **Fair Pay** - Competitive with market for role and experience
2. **Transparency** - Clear salary bands and promotion criteria
3. **Performance-Based** - Top performers earn more
4. **Equity** - Equal pay for equal work, regardless of background

## Salary Reviews

### Annual Review

- Conducted in January/February
- Based on previous year performance
- Market adjustment consideration
- Effective April 1 (new fiscal year)

### Promotion-Based

- Salary increase with promotion
- Typically 15-25% increase
- Effective from promotion date

### Market Adjustment

- Annual review of market rates
- Adjustment if significantly below market
- Subject to company performance

---

## Promotion Criteria

### How to Get Promoted

1. **Consistent Performance** - Meet/exceed targets for 6+ months
2. **Skill Demonstration** - Show capability for next level
3. **Leadership** - Help others, improve processes
4. **Business Need** - Role must exist at next level
5. **Manager Recommendation** - Strong support from leadership

### Promotion Timeline

| From | To | Typical Timeline |
|------|----|--------------------|
| Intern | Associate | 3-6 months |
| Associate | Senior | 12-18 months |
| Senior | Lead | 18-24 months |
| Lead | Manager | 24-36 months |

## FAQs

**Q: When is salary review?**
A: Annual review in January/February, effective April 1.

**Q: How do incentives work?**
A: Incentives are calculated monthly and paid with salary.

**Q: What if I'm below market rate?**
A: Discuss with HR. We review and adjust as needed.

**Q: How do I get a raise?**
A: Perform well, demonstrate next-level skills, discuss with manager.

**Q: Are incentives guaranteed?**
A: No. Incentives are earned based on performance.
