## Leave Request Process

### How to Request Leave

1. Submit request through HR system (or email to manager)
2. Include: dates, type of leave, reason (optional for PTO)
3. Request at least:
   - 1 day notice for 1-2 days leave
   - 1 week notice for 3-5 days leave
   - 2 weeks notice for 1+ week leave

### Approval

| Leave Duration | Approver |
|----------------|----------|
| 1-2 days | Direct Manager |
| 3-5 days | Direct Manager + HR notification |
| 1+ week | Manager + Founder |

### Coverage

Before taking leave:
- [ ] Notify team members who need to know
- [ ] Complete or hand off urgent work
- [ ] Set up out-of-office reply
- [ ] Update Slack status
- [ ] Inform key clients (if applicable)

## Leave Types

### Casual Leave

- For personal matters, errands, events
- Can be taken in half-day increments
- Does not carry over to next year (use it or lose it)

### Sick Leave

- For illness, medical appointments
- Notify manager as soon as possible
- Medical certificate required for 3+ consecutive days
- Does not carry over (India) / Carries over up to 10 days (US)

### Earned Leave (India) / PTO (US)

- For vacations, extended time off
- Advance notice required
- Can carry over limited days (check policy)
- Unused days paid out on exit

## Unpaid Leave

### When Applicable

- Extended leave beyond PTO balance
- Personal leave of absence
- Special circumstances (case by case)

### Process

1. Discuss with manager first
2. Submit formal request to HR
3. Approval from Founder required
4. Maximum 30 days unpaid leave

### During Unpaid Leave

- Benefits continue (employee pays share)
- No salary paid
- Position held (up to approved duration)

---

## Leave Restrictions

### Blackout Periods

Leave may be restricted during:
- Critical project deadlines
- End of quarter/year close
- Client emergencies
- Company events

Manager will communicate restrictions in advance.

### Minimum Team Coverage

At least 50% of each team must be working at any time. Coordinate with teammates when planning leave.

---

## Sick Leave Details

### Calling In Sick

1. Notify manager as early as possible (before work hours)
2. Message via Slack or phone
3. Provide estimated return date
4. Update if status changes

### Extended Illness

- 3+ days: Medical certificate required
- 1+ week: Regular check-ins with manager
- Long-term: Discuss leave of absence options

### Mental Health

Mental health days are valid sick days. Take care of yourself.

---

## Work From Home (Sick)

If you're mildly unwell but can work:
- Discuss with manager
- Work from home if possible
- Don't push yourself
- Take sick leave if needed

Unused earned leave/PTO is paid out on resignation/termination.

## FAQs

**Q: Can I take half days?**
A: Yes, casual and sick leave can be taken in half-day increments.

**Q: What if I get sick on vacation?**
A: Contact manager. Sick days may be converted to sick leave (with documentation).

**Q: Can I work during leave?**
A: Leave is for rest. Don't work during leave unless absolutely necessary and approved.

**Q: What if my leave is denied?**
A: Discuss with manager for alternative dates. Escalate to HR if needed.

**Q: Do unused days expire?**
A: Casual leave expires yearly. Other leave has limited carryover (see policy).
