## The Math

### Revenue Model (US IT Staffing)

| Metric | Conservative | Target |
|--------|--------------|--------|
| Avg bill rate | $65/hr | $70/hr |
| Avg pay rate | $55/hr | $55/hr |
| Spread (margin) | $10/hr | $15/hr |
| Monthly margin/placement | $1,700 (~₹1.4L) | $2,500 (~₹2.1L) |

### Break-Even Analysis

**At ₹7L monthly cost:**
- Need ~5 active placements generating margin
- Or ~3-4 new placements/month (with some churn)

**Target by Month 6:**
- 15-20 active placements
- ₹8-12L monthly gross margin
- Team cost covered + profit

## Risk Scenarios

### Scenario A: Slower Ramp (Placements take longer)

| Month | Placements | Revenue | Spend | Net |
|-------|------------|---------|-------|-----|
| 3 | 0 | ₹0 | ₹3L | -₹3L |
| 4 | 1 | ₹1.4L | ₹4L | -₹2.6L |
| 5 | 3 | ₹4.2L | ₹5L | -₹0.8L |
| 6 | 5 | ₹7L | ₹5L | +₹2L |

**Cumulative at Month 6:** -₹12.4L (still within ₹42L budget)
**Break-even:** Month 8-9

**Mitigation:**
- More aggressive bench marketing
- Focus on faster-to-fill roles
- Founder brings more jobs

### Scenario B: Intern Failure (50% drop out)

**Impact:** Need to hire more external recruiters earlier
**Cost increase:** ~₹50K-70K/month
**Mitigation:** Better screening, more candidates in pipeline

### Scenario C: Key Hire Leaves

**Impact:** 1-2 month setback
**Cost:** ₹50K-1L in rehiring costs
**Mitigation:**
- Competitive compensation
- Strong culture
- Backup training for key processes

## Key Milestones & Decision Points

### Month 1 Checkpoint
- [ ] Team Lead performing? (If not, replace fast)
- [ ] Interns showing promise? (Identify weak ones early)
- [ ] Systems working?

### Month 3 Checkpoint
- [ ] First placement achieved?
- [ ] If not: What's blocking? Fix or pivot.
- [ ] Intern conversions on track?

### Month 4 Decision Point
**If 0 placements by Month 4:**
- Evaluate: Is it people, process, or market?
- Consider: Hire experienced closer vs train more
- Option: Slow down hiring, extend runway

### Month 6 Decision Point
**If target met (10+ placements):**
- Accelerate hiring
- Expand team to 15-20
- Add new capabilities (delivery, account management)

**If below target (5-9 placements):**
- Maintain current team
- Focus on efficiency
- Extend timeline to Month 9

**If significantly below (<5 placements):**
- Restructure team
- Identify root cause
- Consider pivoting approach
