### The "Train to Hire" Strategy

**Why this works in India:**
- Large pool of educated graduates seeking entry
- Lower risk to test capabilities
- Build loyalty through investment
- Shape culture from scratch
- Cost-effective talent pipeline

### Intern Hiring Profile

**Target Candidates:**
- Fresh graduates (BBA, MBA, BCom, BA with good communication)
- Tier 2/3 city colleges (hungry, lower expectations)
- Strong English communication (written + verbal)
- Basic computer skills
- Willingness to learn

**Assessment:**
1. Resume screening (education, communication)
2. Written test (email drafting, comprehension)
3. Phone screen (spoken English, attitude)
4. Role play (mock candidate call)
5. Trial task (1-2 day paid project)

### Intern Training Program (12 Weeks)

#### Month 1: Foundation

| Week | Focus | Activities |
|------|-------|------------|
| 1 | Industry 101 | Staffing basics, terminology, how money flows |
| 2 | Tools & Systems | InTime platform, email, LinkedIn basics |
| 3 | Communication | Email templates, phone scripts, professional etiquette |
| 4 | Sourcing 101 | Job boards, LinkedIn, Boolean search |

**Deliverables:**
- Pass written test on industry basics
- Source 50 candidates (any quality)
- Send 20 outreach messages
- Handle 5 supervised calls

#### Month 2: Specialization

| Week | Focus | Activities |
|------|-------|------------|
| 5-6 | Deep Sourcing | Advanced search, quality over quantity |
| 7-8 | Screening | Phone screens, qualification frameworks |

**Deliverables:**
- Source 30 qualified candidates
- Conduct 20 phone screens
- 10 candidates ready for pool

#### Month 3: Independence

| Week | Focus | Activities |
|------|-------|------------|
| 9-10 | Full Cycle | End-to-end ownership of candidates |
| 11-12 | Evaluation | Performance review, role assignment |

**Deliverables:**
- Own 20+ candidates in pipeline
- 2-3 submissions to jobs
- Ready for permanent role or exit

### Conversion Criteria

**Intern → Associate (after 3 months):**
- Meets activity targets consistently
- Shows initiative and ownership
- Communication quality is good
- Culture fit confirmed

**Conversion Package:**
- ₹18,000-22,000/month base
- Performance incentives begin
- Full employee benefits

**Expected Conversion Rate:** 50-70%
- Not everyone will make it - that's okay
- Better to filter early than keep poor fits

## Where to Find Interns

### Recruitment Channels

1. **LinkedIn Jobs** - Post intern positions
2. **Naukri.com** - Fresher category
3. **Internshala** - Dedicated internship platform
4. **College Placement Cells** - Tier 2/3 colleges in Hyderabad
5. **Referrals** - Current employees' networks

### Target Colleges (Hyderabad Region)

**MBA/BBA Programs:**
- VJIM, ICFAI, ISB feeders
- Tier 2 B-schools with placement challenges
- Distance MBA students looking for breaks

**General Graduates:**
- BA/BCom from good colleges
- Strong English, looking for corporate entry
- Career changers

### Selection Process

1. **Application** (Online form)
   - Basic info + English writing sample
   - "Why do you want to work in recruiting?"

2. **Written Test** (30 min)
   - Email drafting exercise
   - Reading comprehension
   - Basic aptitude

3. **Phone Screen** (15 min)
   - English speaking assessment
   - Attitude and motivation
   - Basic questions

4. **Role Play** (20 min)
   - Mock candidate call
   - Assess communication
   - Coachability

5. **Trial Task** (Paid, 1-2 days)
   - Real sourcing assignment
   - See actual work quality
   - Final assessment

## Key Success Factors

### For Intern Model to Work

1. **Strong Team Lead** - Most critical hire; sets the culture
2. **Structured Training** - Can't wing it; needs curriculum
3. **Clear Metrics** - Know what success looks like
4. **Quick Feedback** - Daily coaching in early weeks
5. **Honest Evaluation** - Exit poor fits early, don't drag out
6. **Celebration** - Recognize progress and wins

### For Remote Management (Founder in NA)

1. **Daily Sync** - 30 min overlap call every day
2. **Clear Dashboards** - Real-time visibility into metrics
3. **Trust but Verify** - Empower but audit
4. **Regular Visits** - Quarterly in-person time
5. **Strong India Lead** - Your proxy on the ground
