## Risk Assessment & Mitigation

### Key Risks

**1. Students Don't Get Placed (Market Risk)**
- **Likelihood:** Medium (30% in bad market)
- **Impact:** High (reputation damage)
- **Mitigation:**
  - Job placement conditional on certification (manage expectations)
  - Partner with recruiting pod (internal advantage)
  - Transparency about placement rates

**2. AI Costs Spike (Technology Risk)**
- **Likelihood:** Low (but possible)
- **Impact:** Medium (margin compression)
- **Mitigation:**
  - High-ticket industry (can justify costs)
  - Continuous optimization (already at $0.29/user)
  - Pass cost increases to customers if needed

**3. Key Employees Leave (Operational Risk)**
- **Likelihood:** Medium
- **Impact:** High (revenue loss)
- **Mitigation:**
  - 100% process-oriented (documented)
  - AI captures institutional knowledge
  - Client relationships distributed across team

**4. Competitor Copies Model (Competitive Risk)**
- **Likelihood:** High (inevitable)
- **Impact:** Medium (market share pressure)
- **Mitigation:**
  - First-mover advantage (speed)
  - Proprietary AI (learns from our data)
  - Network effects (more users = better product)
  - Constant innovation

**5. Regulatory Compliance (Legal Risk)**
- **Likelihood:** Low (manageable)
- **Impact:** Medium (potential fines)
- **Mitigation:**
  - Partner with experts (immigration lawyers, CAs)
  - Industry-standard compliance (not aggressive)
  - Transparency with customers
